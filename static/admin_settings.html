LLM Model Configuration

Select the primary LLM. Models ending in "-latest" are generally recommended for the most up-to-date version. "Flash" models are optimized for speed and cost, while "Pro" models offer a balance of performance and capability. Avoid models with "exp" (experimental) or specific version numbers (e.g., -001) for production use. For more details, refer to the official Gemini Models documentation.
